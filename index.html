About Me

Hi! My name is Gordon Lei, a current undergraduate studying computer science at New York University Tandon School of Engineering. I have worked as a TA for a summer Object-Oriented Programming course at Tandon during the summer of 2019. I was sadly not able to participate in an internship over this past summer (summer of 2020), I spent this summer taking online courses/resources to learn new tools that I've always heard about but never really used before, such as Docker and Node.js. In this time of COVID-19, I've also had the opportunity to pursue interests other than computer science, mainly cooking. I can't make restaurant-worthy food, but it's a start! Currently, I am taking a course that includes the topics of or is named introduction to Databases, Intro to Operating Systems, Software Engineering, and Artificial Intelligence.
